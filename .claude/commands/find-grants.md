Help the operator find grant opportunities for LCAC.

1. Read `docs/ai-workflow-upgrade/lcac-knowledge-base.md` for LCAC's mission, programs, location, and who they serve.
2. Use built-in web search to find CURRENT grant opportunities that fit, focusing on:
   - Autism / developmental disability / family-support funders
   - Lewis County WA, Washington State, and Pacific Northwest community/regional funders
   - Small-nonprofit and rural-community grants
   - Funders aligned to specific LCAC programs (SDCC center, Summer Sensory Camp, Court Support, Cultivating Belonging, etc.)
3. For each promising grant, give: funder name, what it funds, who's eligible, rough award range, **deadline**, and a link.
4. Sort by deadline, soonest first. Clearly flag anything closing within 30 days.
5. Offer to start a first draft for any of them with `/draft-grant`.

Use ONLY built-in web search. Do not sign up for, log into, or call any paid service. If you're unsure a grant is current, say so and point her to verify on the funder's site.
