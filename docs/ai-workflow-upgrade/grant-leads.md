# Grant Leads for Lewis County Autism Coalition (LCAC)

These are real, verifiable grant opportunities LCAC could realistically apply for, gathered **as of June 2026 — always verify deadlines, amounts, and eligibility on the funder's own site before applying.** Funder programs change, windows open and close, and some "rolling" programs pause without notice. Every entry links to a source URL. Where something looked uncertain or possibly out of date, it is flagged in the notes.

LCAC profile used for fit: a rural Lewis County, WA nonprofit serving autistic individuals and families; programs include a physical community center (SDCC), a summer sensory camp, court/legal support, community education/inclusion, a regional autism conference, and support groups.

> Note on eligibility: a few well-known autism programs (Ben's Fund, ASDF Summer Scholarships, ACT Today) fund **families/individuals directly, not organizations.** They are listed at the bottom under "Family-direct funds to share with families" because LCAC can point families to them even though LCAC itself can't be the applicant.

## ⚠️ Closing within ~90 days (by end of August 2026) — act first

These have the nearest deadlines. Prioritize them.

- **USDA Rural Business Development Grants** — deadline **June 30, 2026** (June 15 for SECD applications). Nonprofits serving rural areas eligible; Lewis County qualifies as rural. Verify your project type fits "business/community development." [Source](https://www.rd.usda.gov/programs-services/business-programs/rural-business-development-grants-31)
- **Anderson Foundation for Autism Grant** — application window **June 1–30, 2026** (very narrow). 501(c)(3)s improving quality of life for autistic individuals; $500–$5,000 typical. [Source](https://www.andersoncenterforautism.org/afagrants/)
- **WA Developmental Disabilities Council — LEIT Program** — proposals due **July 23, 2026, 4:59pm PST.** Large multi-year award ($120k/yr); focused on policy/advocacy voice for people with developmental disabilities. Ambitious for LCAC's size — consider partnering. Requires a WEBs account. [Source](https://www.ddc.wa.gov/post/notice-of-funding-opportunity-legislative-engagement-information-tools-leit-program)
- **Ben's Fund (next quarterly window opens July 6, 2026)** — *family-direct, not for LCAC as applicant* — but timely to share with families this quarter. [Source](https://www.bensfund.org/apply-to-our-grant/)
- **Walmart Spark Good Local Grants — Cycle 2** closes **July 15, 2026** (then Cycle 3: Aug 1–Nov 30). Apply at your local store; $250–$5,000. [Source](https://www.walmart.org/how-we-give/program-guidelines/spark-good-local-grants-guidelines)
