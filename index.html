Understanding

What Is Autism?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurological and developmental difference that affects how people communicate, learn, behave, and interact with the world. Autism is not a disease or something to be "fixed" - it is a natural part of human diversity.

People on the autism spectrum have unique strengths, perspectives, and contributions. At LCAC, we promote autism acceptance and work to create environments where everyone can thrive.
