Our Mission

Belonging at the Heart of It All

Fostering belonging is at the heart of our mission. With the support of a diverse network of community partners, we lead county-wide education and awareness campaigns to promote acceptance and empower Lewis County residents with intellectual, behavioral, physical and developmental differences.

Our efforts are designed to create positive impacts in schools, workplaces, and the broader community, enhancing overall well-being.
