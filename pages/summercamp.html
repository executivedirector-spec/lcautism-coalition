Important

Parent / Caregiver On-Site

This is a parent/caregiver participation or on-site supervision program. While our team will lead structured activities and provide guidance throughout each session, we kindly ask that a parent or guardian remain onsite or within close proximity (such as the parking lot) during camp hours. This approach ensures each child's safety, comfort, and individualized support if needed.

What's Happening

Activities & Learning

A blend of educational opportunities for parents and engaging hands-on activities for kids.

Parent Education Component

This year, we're planning to add a parent education component featuring guest speakers from local organizations who will share valuable resources and knowledge to support families.

Fun Activities

As in previous years, we'll be joined by community partners offering a variety of engaging sessions. These may include yoga, martial arts, creative arts, dance, and more!

Funding Option

Peer Mentorship Available

For eligible participants, our programs may qualify as peer mentorship under WA State Developmental Disabilities Community Services. With approved DDCS authorization, your child can have a dedicated staff person for support during camp.
