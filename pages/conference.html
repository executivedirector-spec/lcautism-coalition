Important Update

Returning November 2026

The Lewis County Autism Coalition made the strategic decision to pause the 15th Annual Southwest Washington Autism Conference last year so we could focus on launching our first-ever physical space, the Spectrum & Development Community Center, while juggling new contracts, expanded programming, and the broader operational responsibilities that come with growth.

The board agreed that protecting quality, mission alignment, and long-term sustainability is ultimately the best way to serve our community, even when that means making tough calls. The conference is back on the calendar for November 2026 — details, speakers, and registration will go up here as soon as they are confirmed. In the meantime, we remain deeply committed to meaningful engagement.
