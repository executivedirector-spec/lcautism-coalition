Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

The Lewis County Autism Coalition is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with us:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number when you contact us, submit a form, or sign up for updates
  • Mailing address if you request printed materials or correspondence
  • Volunteer application details, including availability, skills, and background information
  • Donation records as required by federal and state nonprofit regulations

We only collect information that is necessary to serve you and fulfill our mission. We do not collect data from children without parental consent.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, questions, and requests for support
  • Process volunteer applications and coordinate volunteer activities
  • Send you updates about our programs, events, and services -- only if you have opted in to receive communications
  • Process and acknowledge donations, and provide tax receipts
  • Improve our programs, services, and website experience

We will never use your information for purposes unrelated to our mission without your explicit consent.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

In limited circumstances, we may share information with:

  • The Department of Children, Youth, and Families (DCYF), the Developmental Disabilities Administration (DDA), or DSHS as required for contracted services we provide
  • Service providers who assist us with website hosting, email communications, or payment processing -- under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement or government agencies when required by law or court order

Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Our practices include:

  • Following HIPAA guidelines for any protected health information entrusted to us
  • Compliance with the Washington State Public Records Act (RCW 42.56), including applicable exemptions that protect personal and sensitive information
  • Using secure, encrypted connections for data transmission on our website
  • Limiting access to personal information to authorized staff and volunteers on a need-to-know basis
  • Storing form submissions in a secure, access-controlled database that requires staff sign-in to view

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us immediately if you believe your information has been compromised.

Cookies

Our website uses basic cookies to ensure proper site functionality, such as remembering your preferences and supporting navigation. These cookies are essential for the website to operate and do not track you for advertising purposes.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. If we use any analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site, the data collected is anonymized and used solely to improve the user experience.

You can control cookie settings through your web browser preferences at any time.

Contact for Privacy Concerns

If you have questions about this privacy policy, would like to request access to your personal data, or wish to have your information removed from our records, please contact us:

  • Email: info@lcautism.org
  • Phone: (360) 644-5222
  • Mail: Lewis County Autism Coalition, 1673 S. Market St. PMB #240, Chehalis, WA 98532

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

Nonprofit Status

The Lewis County Autism Coalition is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit organization. Our Employer Identification Number (EIN) is 47-3931045. We are registered with the Washington Secretary of State's Charities Program and comply with all applicable state and federal regulations governing nonprofit organizations.

This privacy policy was last updated on January 1, 2026. We may update this policy from time to time and will post any changes on this page.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices or would like more information about the Lewis County Autism Coalition, we are happy to help.
