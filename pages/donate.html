How Your Donation Helps

Every contribution directly supports programs and services for our community.

🎓

SMART Program

Funds early identification and coordinated support for children through the School-Medical Autism Review Team.

🤝

Social Groups

Supports teen and adult social groups that build friendships, life skills, and a sense of belonging.

☀️

Summer Camp

Provides welcoming recreation and sensory-friendly activities for neurodiverse youth every summer.

🏠

Community Center

Maintains and grows the Spectrum & Development Community Center as a hub for programs and connection.

Your Generosity Changes Lives
