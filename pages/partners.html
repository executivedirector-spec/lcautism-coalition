Our Network

Who Makes This Possible

At the Lewis County Autism Coalition, we believe in the power of collaboration to create lasting impact. Our community partners play a vital role in our mission to support individuals with autism and developmental disabilities. By joining forces with local businesses, educational institutions, healthcare providers, and other nonprofit organizations, we are able to provide comprehensive services and resources that enhance the quality of life for those we serve. Together, we foster an inclusive community where everyone can thrive.
